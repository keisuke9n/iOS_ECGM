ECG Monitor SIM

App Overview

ECG Monitor SIM is a minimalist ECG simulator and quiz app designed for learning and practicing cardiac rhythms. It provides a clean monitor view, realistic wave motion, and focused training tools without distractions.

Key Features

  • Real-time ECG waveform rendering on a monitor-style grid
  • Rhythms: Sinus Rhythm, Atrial Fibrillation, Ventricular Fibrillation, Asystole
  • Adjustable heart rate in SR and AF
  • Modifiers: ST Depression, Negative T wave
  • Quiz mode with 4 live waveforms per question
  • Round-based scoring with feedback, sound, and haptics
  • Optimized for both iPhone and iPad

Intended Use

ECG Monitor SIM is for educational and demonstration purposes only. The waveforms are synthetic and do not represent real patient data. The app is not a medical device and must not be used for diagnosis or treatment.
